# rarecube.tv

> The New Internet Television — a live-streaming platform fused with Solana creator-coin mechanics.

Twitch-shaped streaming — RTMP in from OBS, live video out — with crypto-native economics: creator coins and in-platform swaps (bags.fm, Helius). Runs on **self-hosted LiveKit** (WebRTC) with **OvenMedia** for HLS distribution on Hetzner infrastructure — sub-second latency, not a hosted video API.

Second act of a decade in streaming: the first was [Yungcloud](#/yungcloud) (2015–18, 20k users, SF Weekly-featured).
