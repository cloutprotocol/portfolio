# Spatial Computing & Immersive Video

> visionOS systems work: spatial video pipelines, photogrammetry, gaussian splats, immersive playback.

Developed spatial video codec conversion pipelines, photogrammetry processing, gaussian splat rendering, and immersive video playback systems for **visionOS** — plus on-device NLP/ML analysis, speech synthesis/recognition, and haptics experiments (see [Max](#/max)).

Swift, SwiftUI, RealityKit, CoreML, AVFoundation, Metal, ImmersiveSpace.

The work ended when the Vision Pro was lost in the 2024 Altadena fire.
