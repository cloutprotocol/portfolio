# bitmap.wtf

> A Bitmap metaverse explorer, live within ~two weeks of the protocol's launch. Summer 2023.

Bitmap turned Bitcoin blocks into claimable land; bitmap.wtf explored it. The site's own meta tag read *"bitmap.wtf by @ordinalos."*
