# Thunder Horse Video

> "The production powerhouse… art, video, fog, laser and light projection." — *The New York Times, T Magazine*, 2011

Co-founded the Brooklyn collective — producing immersive live experiences and installations, pioneering real-time CG visuals in live performance.

From the [T Magazine feature](https://archive.nytimes.com/tmagazine.blogs.nytimes.com/2011/07/22/the-jam-thunder-horse-video/) (July 2011):

> "Thunder Horse in the next year will probably do visuals for some of the most major acts in music… Their sensibility really straddles so many genres between gothic and tropical, with a sense of humor interspersed."

![Ford & Lopatin — directed by Thunder Horse Video and Tabor Robak](https://vimeo.com/video/24113258)
*Ford & Lopatin (ex-Games / Oneohtrix Point Never) — the video featured in T Magazine, co-directed with Tabor Robak*

## selected work

- **SALEM at the Delano pool, Art Basel Miami 2011** — full performance design, presented by [The Hole](https://thehole.com/fairs/the-hole-delano-present-salem): *"a surreal swatch of fog, light, and effects… a stage designed for the pool of the Delano"*
- **MoMA PS1** — stage installations for Warm Up 2013 and the Future Brown commission (VW Sunday Sessions, DIS Magazine collaboration)
- **Museum of Arts and Design** — immersive cinema installation, NYC Makers Biennial 2014
- Ongoing collaborations with **SALEM** (internationally) and **Gatekeeper**; Art Basel productions for Matthew Stone, Kelela, Zebra Katz, boychild
- Tour production — lighting and FX — for **Yung Lean**'s Unknown Memory tour (2014)
