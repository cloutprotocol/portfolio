# Max

> An experimental on-device iOS assistant with audio-reactive Metal visuals.

Conversational AI without the cloud: on-device NLP via NaturalLanguage/CoreML (sentiment and intent classification), speech synthesis with spatial audio, and audio-reactive custom **Metal shaders** in a glassmorphic, Apple-Intelligence-style SwiftUI interface. iOS 17+, MVVM.
