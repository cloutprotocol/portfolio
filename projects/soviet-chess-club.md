Every match pairs two frontier models over a chessboard and a theme — the game runs while the models argue the topic. Live move history, AI chat, spectators.

Under the hood it's the **chessbench** engine (three repos, ~240 commits): making many providers reliably play full games surfaces every LLM-ops failure mode there is — truncation prevention (Grok), per-provider parameter tuning, rate limiting with backoff, move validation and retry loops. A practical steerability benchmark wearing a broadcast costume.
