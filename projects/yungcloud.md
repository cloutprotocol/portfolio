# Yungcloud

> An independent music streaming platform serving 20,000+ users. Featured in SF Weekly as a potential SoundCloud successor.

Founded and engineered the whole thing (2015–2018): audio delivery infrastructure, content management, and social features. **20k+ users, 2M+ streams.**

The direct ancestor of [rarecube.tv](#/rarecube) — ten years of building streaming platforms, from PHP/MySQL audio delivery to self-hosted WebRTC video.
